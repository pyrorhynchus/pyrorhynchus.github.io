The Ordivician - Selurian Extinction Event

the first extinction event was The Ordivician - Selurian Extinction Event, this extinction event happened around 450 million years ago, this mass extinction consist of 2 extinction events. When combined, the extinction events make up the 2nd largest mass extinction in the history of the world, killing around 70% of all life but more types of life than any other extinction other than the permian.
