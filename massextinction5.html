The Cretaceous - Paleogene Extinction

The Cretaceous - Paleogene extinction is the most well known of the big 5, this is the most discussed extinction event in modern science, for people are still not 100% sure on the cause, the The cretaceous - Paleogene extinction, or K-PG for short, occurred around 66 million years ago, this event saw around 75% of life going extinct, the reason why the extinction occured is still widely discussed, but a few of the most prominent predictions include,
